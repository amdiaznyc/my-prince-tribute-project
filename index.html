Hello, World! Welcome to my Prince Tribute Page!!!

This is my tribute project to one of the greatest musical artists, the late, great Prince Rogers Nelson.

Growing up from humble beginnings in Minneanapolis, Minnesota, Prince rose to become an icon and legend in the music business.

Fast Facts:

  • Prince Rogers Nelson was born on June 7th, 1958 and died on April 21st, 2016.
  • Prince was an American singer-songwriter, musician, record producer, dancer, actor and filmmaker.
  • He was known for his prolific output, releasing 39 albums during his life and a vast array of unreleased projects left in a vault at his home.
